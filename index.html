Very tasty Nicaraguan food, I would definitely recommend. We had the fritanga, and carne Asada as our entrees- which were both delicious, authentic and filling (good portion size). For dessert, we had buñuelos and pilloquinto, both yummy but the buñuelos were our favorite. Exquisite taste!

The ambiance is nice, fun, and exciting. Overall it is a family oriented restaurant with superb service, I would come back any day as it does have a true taste of the Nicaraguan cuisine- reminding me of my travels in Nicaragua.
